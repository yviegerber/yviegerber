Projects

Speech Recognition Calculator

A speech recognition algorithm designed as a spoken calculator by leveraging time and frequency domain analysis and mathematical grammar.

Synesthesia & FSO: Using frequency encoding to visually represent and communicate audio

An intuitive, invertible function that maps the characteristics of an audio file into a visual image. The encoded image transmits the audio via free space optical communication, the captured visual is decoded, and the reconstructed audio is played.

Social-technical Power Relations

My sociology capstone paper investigates the duality that technology provides in both enabling freedom in a digital dimension and the ability to collectivize, alter the narrative of truth, and fight for freedom in a physical dimension.

Precious Life

An essay exploring the primitive desire to learn and the philosophical structures that hold education in society, inspired by my experience within the alternative model of education I experienced in the Johnston Center for Integrative Studies.

Psycho-Subjective Image

Used a method of photo emulsion printmaking to create a conceptual critique of objectivity and subjectivity in modern medicine. A final project for a Medical Imaging course. I took, edited, and printed the photo of myself as a self-portrait.
